--- layout: post title: Burgerlords - Best Vegan Burger in LA date: '2016-05-11T01:50:00.001+02:00' author: Rachel Magasweran tags: - Los Angeles - Food Reviews - Vegan - downtown LA modified_time: '2016-05-11T01:50:07.760+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8588809848435371494.post-8440718123941699411 blogger_orig_url: http://www.robotnyc.com/2016/05/burgerlords-best-vegan-burger-in-la.html --- Burgerlords is my new favorite burger joint! The Vegan Cheese burger is the best veg burger I've ever had, hands down. It's a homemade patty with eggplant, garbanzo beans, barley, leeks, celery and secret spices. They top it with Follow Your Heart cheese and thousand island! (note: FYH cheese and sauce is legit, it melts, and order extra thousand island for the fries.) Everything tastes similar to In-N-Out but without the dead animal inside to ruin it. Try it out!
